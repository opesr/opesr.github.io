Going to #DC #FED Rally Point

Location

Submitted by OpESR on Tue, 2011-06-14 14:00

Remain on sidewalk (Not a street protest). This is not an event intended for entry of federal or private property. When at the federal building take care to avoid obstruction of vehicle entryways to federal property (these are typically guardposted). Please note that if you are taking photographs or filming the event, DHS (Department of Homeland Security) regulations allow you to take imagery from a public way such as a sidewalk, of the exterior of federal building, and the surrounding area. Move away from the federal building if guard staff tell you to or if they indicate a desire to create a buffer between walking protesters and the building.
