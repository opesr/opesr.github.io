The #OpESR Call to Occupy ~ #OpPaperStorm this.

Submitted by sysbreach on Tue, 2011-10-11 00:57

Q: What is #A99? and OpESR?

A: A non-violent social movement with this list of demands: End the campaign finance and lobbying racket, Break up the banks, Enforce RICO laws against the organized criminal class, and Order Ben Bernanke to step down. 

 

The catatonic masses are currently living and toiling for the aggrandizement of an oligarchic plutocracy, that is protected with patriarchal violence, under an ambiguous and ecologically destructive notion called “civilization”. As a result, matriarchal violence has been preemptively muted out, and the masses have become both swollen in number and servile in demeanor.This oligarchic plutocracy has always manifested itself in various ways through out “modern civilization”. It has currently manifested itself in western civilization as follows:

The economic top one-tenth of one percent of  the population has launched an economic war against us. They have consolidated tens of trillions of dollars. We now have the highest and  most severe inequality of wealth in history. While there are a record  number of people currently living paycheck to paycheck, in debt,  unemployed, underemployed, without healthcare, on food stamps and in  poverty; as our society is breaking down, global bankers have taken our  tax dollars and given themselves all-time record-breaking bonuses. The  same people who destroyed our economy have been given trillions of  dollars in national wealth. (*Source:http://ampedstatus.org/network/about/) ☜

Some definitions, as are used in this manifesto:

  • Violence - Physical force exerted for the purpose of violating, damaging, or abusing. Abusive or unjust exercise of power. Abuse or injury to meaning, content, or intent. These definitions of violence serve as the basis for our usage. A distinction between matriarchal violence and patriarchal violence needs to be made for practical purposes.
    • Patriarchal Violence - An illegitimate, pre-emptive, and coercive violence and the constant threat thereof. One of the aims of patriarchal violence is to subsume and control matriarchal violence, as a threat to its existence.The State is the embodiment of patriarchal violence.
    • Matriarchal Violence - A legitimate, responsive, and protective violence and the threat thereof. Matriarchal violence operates in the service of preservation of self, and by extension preservation of species. Directly related to this is a legitimate pursuance  of preservation of individual self-determination and by extension, preservation of social harmony. 
  • Oligarchy - Government by a few, especially by a small faction of persons or families, and those who make up this government.
  • Plutocracy - Government by the wealthy. *Special message from the Democracy, to the Plutocracy http://uleak.it/?93u ☜
  • Civilization - A human society that has highly developed material and spiritual resources and a complex cultural, political, and legal organization; an advanced state in social development. Cities or populated areas, as contrasted with sparsely inhabited areas, deserts, etc…
  • City (urban center) - A large and densely populated urban area; may include several independent administrative districts. Tacit in the definition of the city is the necessity of resource importation and trade for existence, which is supported by the threat and deliverance of patriarchal violence. 
  • Cartel - Also called: trust. A collusive international association of independent enterprises formed to monopolize production and distribution of a product or service, control prices, etc…

Thus, an oligarchic plutocracy is government by the wealthy few, enabling them to control the purchasing power of the many. Its current (and sometimes historical) manifestations are:

  • Preemption of individual self-determination and basic self-sufficiency by inserting itself between all streams of resource acquisition by the individual and the group
  • Commoditization of basic human needs
  • Inserting itself in the middle/co-option of most streams of communication (transportation, verbal and written communication, media et al…)  between individuals
  • Enforcing compliance with the threat and/or commission of disproportionate patriarchal violence
  • Creating an illusory sense of false need via marketing
  • Funding and growing a managerial class
  • Marginalizing dissent through co-opted media channels
  • Reinforcing competitiveness between people by forcing them to comply with a system that exploits scarcity, displacing natural cooperation
  • Artificially holding back technological and scientific advances in the service of profit
  • Providing minimal environmental and waste management controls due to profit-maximization and capitalism’s scarcity-exploiting nature
  • Necessitating a police state to protect property of exploitative class. This represents a local muting out of matriarchal violence.
  • Necessitating a military state to forcibly procure resources needed to grow states that are controlled by the oligarchic plutocrats. 
  • Destroying natural human empathy in the favor of individual selfishness. There is an inverse relationship between success in a capitalist system and human empathy, either as a by-product or by revision. This is because of the preemption of individual self-determination/self-sufficiency. Under a capitalist system, where cooperation is subordinated to competition, people are forced to work in order to acquire the resources needed to ensure their own long-term individual survival. They are forced to chose between having their needs for survival/illusory needs as marketed at them fulfilled via economic proxy (in order to bolster the power of the oligarchs), and being able to look out for others, economically at the expense of their own survival. In the natural order, where competition is subordinated  to cooperation, people are able to meet their needs for survival in a more direct and self-sufficient way, affording them the time and freedom to help others, which would not necessarily be at the expense of their own survival. The latter is arguably preferable because it takes into account both the social necessity of human and animal nature, and the individual’s freedom to live as one chooses.

This brand of hierarchical structure is an inevitable by-product of an oligarchic representative government paired with unchecked scarcity-exploiting value system known as capitalism. This capitalistic system necessarily recognizes resource-hoarding as a virtue, at the expense of ecology. This is protected by the use of both publicly funded militaries and police coupled with private security forces, private sector manipulation and cartel collusion. It should be noted: ecology comes prior to humanity. Ecology is not a subset of humanity, humanity is a subset of the ecological system.

Which Came First? The Chicken or The Egg?

In order to defeat this exploitive and destructive oligarchic plutocracy under which we are all ill-kept slaves, it is necessary to take a philosophical approach, and then marry this with practicality as the situation on the ground dictates. In other words, it is essential to recognize what thing is a result of what other thing(s), why and how this is so, and then to enumerate them in their causal order. Only after we have done this, can we truly tear down and rebuild a broken system and preempt it’s regrowth in the future.
